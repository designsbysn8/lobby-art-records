LA019: Isabel Crespo Pardo - "el (des)cubierto"

Release: 6/30/23

el rostro (des)cubierto is a collection of eight deeply intimate pieces oscillating between composition & improvisation to explore self & sociality. The development of this music required embracing the challenge of patiently listening to one another, striving towards interdependence, & reveling in all that it requires. we crave the kind of unity that retains the individual voice, like a single tree’s rustling melodies, gently/violently shifting in the wind. we’re tracing back & reaching out through weeks & weeks & weeks of gathering to play & talk & try, envisioning all the different pathways we could take through these moments-made-song. & so we offer this: 40 minutes of wondering & wandering with hand over heart, aching in solitude, giving & craving forgiveness, marveling at tiny things, & always always putting cardamom in our tea.

Bio
Isabel Crespo Pardo (they/them) is a NYC-based latinx vocalist, improviser-composer, and interdisciplinary artist. Rooted in conceptual clarity, their work actively entangles music, visual art, text and performance, always evolving to reflect the intra/interpersonal spaces they inhabit. Reveling in soft chaos, they embrace openness and specificity to create poetic work(s).
